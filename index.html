Rules

1. Be Respectful: Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination will not be tolerated.

2. No Spamming: Avoid spamming messages, links, or emojis in the chat. Keep the conversation meaningful and respectful.

3. Stay on Topic: Please make sure your messages are relevant to the channel you’re in. Use the appropriate channels for different discussions.

4. No Advertising: Do not promote other servers, products, or services without permission from the staff team.

5. Listen to Staff: Follow the instructions of the server moderators and admins. They are here to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone.

6. Have Fun: Enjoy your time on the server and contribute to a positive atmosphere!
